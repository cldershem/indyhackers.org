    Holiday Social 2013

    Celebrate the year with your fellow hackers over food and drink!

    Please join us — a bunch of programmers, nerds, geeks, hackers — to celebrate the year with food, drink, and friends both old and new. December 7th, 7PM, at The Speak Easy in Broad Ripple, we will have the third annual Indy Hackers' Holiday Social. We welcome everyone -- Pythonistas, Javanese, COBOLians, Scalanites, PHPers, Rubyists, .NET nuts, Perlsians.... We might even have some hardcore functional programming zealots giving in to mutable state for a few hours. The best part? It is completely free.

    Update! You can't join us because this event is over. Like, WAY over. You can relive the excitement by checking out the pictures from the event, as captured by official Holiday Social documentarian, @thirdshift.

  • What's Happening?

    As usual, expect food, drink, and prizes. As we promised last year, this year is going to be bigger and better.

    • Food

      Enjoy some delectable barbecue from local favorite GT South's! If you're vegetarian, we've got you covered, too: veggie hyerollers, an olive and hummus tray, a veggie tray, and a fruit tray. There will be plenty of food, so come hungry! Brought to you by Bradley and Montgomery.

    • Drink

      For those of age, 1 keg of Wee Mac, 1 keg of Suncream Light Ale, and 1 keg of Osiris Pale Ale from Sun King. Do you have a strong preference for wine, whiskey, or something else over beer? Bug us on Twitter. (Don't worry if you don't drink! There will also be soft drinks.) All of this is brought to you by our sponsor Iasta!

    • Games

      This year, let's play together! We might not all be gamers regularly, but playing Super Mario Kart against friends is almost always fun. What about some NBA Jam (original!)? BOOM SHAKALAKA! How about some board games? Puerto Rico will be ready for your rule. We might even have available a copy of Kickstarter's best selling board game of all time, Robot Turtles. (Don't worry, there's a set of rules for big people.)
